Sports Store Management

  • Customer Management

    Customer Management is a tool that is used to manage the customer details.The customers are people who buys things in the Sports Store.Seller can manage their details.

  • Item Management

    Item Management is a pool of Sport's items that has all items to belonging to the different sports.Seller can Manage the items and can manually edit the items in the item Management controller

  • Order Management

    Order Management contains the orders received by the Sports store website.Those orders are ordered by various people throgh out the country. Seller can view that orders and can do their opeartions accordingly.
